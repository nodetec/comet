# Lunar South Pole Water

The water story keeps getting simplified into a yes or no question, but the real challenge is distribution.
There is ice, there are trapped grains, there are shadows that preserve more than the adjacent slope, and there are ridges where extraction costs erase most of the easy optimism.
Our mapping run covered fourteen kilometers and turned one promising basin into three modest targets.

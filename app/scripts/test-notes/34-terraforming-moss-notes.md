# Terraforming Moss Notes

#science/botany #mars research#

The moss trays are not terraforming in any practical sense, but nobody on the botany team has the energy to argue with branding anymore.
What they are doing is useful: binding dust, holding moisture, and proving that a controlled patch can survive more freeze-thaw cycles than last year.
Progress in hostile places often looks embarrassingly small until it suddenly looks obvious.
